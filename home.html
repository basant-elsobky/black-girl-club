WHY BLACK WOMEN AND BUSINESS?

Recent growth in the rate of business ownership by Black women has been faster than any other group in the nation.

From 2002-11, the number of businesses owned by Black women increased 179% compared with 52% for all women-owned businesses and 20% for all businesses. However, many Black women who own businesses have difficulty accessing credit and face capital constraints..
